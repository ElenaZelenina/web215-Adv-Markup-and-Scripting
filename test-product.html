Bender Mini Figure

Mega Construx Heroes Series 2

Highlights

  • Highly collectible, super-poseable Bender micro action figure
  • Premium printed detail for deluxe display
  • Figure comes with authentic robot oil can and money bag accessories
  • Includes buildable nameplate display stand with printed name detail
  • Released February 2018

Description

It’s gonna be fun on the bun with this deluxe, buildable Bender figure! Bite my shiny metal bricks! Now you, chumps and chumpettes, can build your own collectible Bender micro action figure, complete with deluxe details and premium printed decoration. Based on the scheming Futurama character, this super-poseable figure comes with authentic Mom’s Old-Fashioned Robot Oil and money bag accessories, as well as a buildable stand with printed name detail for the ultimate display. Neat! Build and collect all the 6 in the series, meatbag.
